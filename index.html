About fossapps

Fossapps is a Magisk Module that installs free and open source alternative to Google Apps on to your phone. Just like the apps included on Fossapps,Fossapps is fully free and open source

Apps included on Fossapps

QKMSM to replace Messages
Koler to replace Phone and Contacts
Lawnchair to replace Pixel Launcher
Quickswitch to enable Gesture support for Lawnchair
Etar to replace Calendar
Metro Music to replace Music
Kiwi to replace Chrome
K9-Mail to replace Gmail
Ncalc to replace Calculator
Open Camera to replace Camera
F-Droid to Install or update more Foss Apps
NewPipe to replace Youtube

What are the different versions?

Base:

The base version does not include MicroG, so it can run properly on devices with Google Services already installed

MicroG Support:

The MicroG support version does what the name says, it included MicroG for devices without any Google apps

Full system app replace (BETA):

The full system app replace replaces system apps, rather than installing along side them

Full System app replace With MicroG Support (BETA)

The full system app replace with MicroG Support contains all of the MicroG apps, for devices without any Google Apps installed


Addons

Fossapps Addons are modules that add more to the normal Fossapps Installation

Google Client Addon (GCA)

Installs Aurora Store and Vanced Manager, Free and Open Source alternatives to the Google Play Store and Youtube.
